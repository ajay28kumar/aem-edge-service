Harley Quinn

Harley Quinn is a former psychiatrist turned antihero known for unpredictability and emotional complexity.
Harley Quinn began her career as a medical professional before becoming involved in Gotham’s criminal underworld.
She is characterized by her energetic personality, independence, and evolving moral alignment. Over time, she transitions from villain to antihero.
Harley Quinn’s stories often explore identity, autonomy, and emotional resilience.
Her character balances humor with serious themes of personal growth.
